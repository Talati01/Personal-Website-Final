Intro

My Name is Talati Polamalu born and raised in Southern, Oregon. I have been living in Washington D.C for the past 6 years. I built this website in hopes to showcase my life more creatively and showcase some of the work that I am proud of.

Over the past five years, I've dedicated myself to a journey of academic excellence at the prestigious Georgetown University. Last May marked a significant milestone as I proudly received my Bachelor's Degree in Sociology, a testament to my passion for understanding societal dynamics and human behavior. Now, as I eagerly anticipate this coming May, I am on the cusp of achieving yet another milestone: the completion of my Master's in Management from Georgetown. It's been a fulfilling and enriching experience, one that has not only deepened my knowledge but also honed my skills for effective leadership and strategic decision-making. Georgetown has provided me with an exceptional education and a platform to thrive, and I'm excited to embark on the next chapter of my journey armed with the invaluable insights and expertise gained from my time here.

Hobbies

What do I do in my freetime? Im glad you asked!!

Athletics

I have been an athlete my entire life and I am very competitive by nature. I played for Georgetown Universities football team between 2019-2024 on the offensive line and have recieved all league honors in doing so. Before college I participated in baseball, basketball and tennis as well. I enjoy the community and family dynamic athletics brings you a part of and will forever value the lessons and achievements that athletics have helped me get to.

Outdoors

Growing up in the beautiful state of Oregon, I fell in love with the outdoors at an early age. When I get the chance to go back home I enjoy being outside in the sun as much as possible (oregon has about 4 months of sun a year). I like spending time out on the water with my family, hiking around the PNW with my friends, and spending some much needed alone time fishing around secret spots I wont disclose the name of. I pride myself on being an outdoorsman at heart and enjoying the little things life has to offer.

Video Games & Tech

In my freetime I also enjoy to play video games and learn about new technology. I grew up in a household with 2 older brothers and we bonded over sharing mutliple gaming consoles throughout our youth. I enjoy playing games with my friends and being able to get my mind off of everything going on in my hectic life. As it goes for technology I am very passionate about photography and videography, I recently purchased a new camera and have been learning how to turn this passion into a side hustle I can build on over time.
